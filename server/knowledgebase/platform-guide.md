# Afterma Platform — Knowledge Base Guide

## Overview
Afterma is a comprehensive maternal health management platform. This guide covers all major features and how to use them effectively.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Download the Afterma app or visit the web platform.
2. Sign up with your mobile number (OTP-based authentication).
3. Complete your health profile: name, expected due date or delivery date, type of delivery, and any health conditions.
4. Add your doctor's information if you wish to share health data with them.

### Setting Up Your Profile
Your profile stores your:
- **Pregnancy / Delivery information**: Due date, actual delivery date, delivery type (normal/C-section)
- **Health conditions**: Pre-existing conditions like diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders
- **Medication list**: Current prescriptions and vitamins
- **Doctor details**: Linked healthcare provider

---

## 2. Health Tracking Features

### Daily Health Log
Log your daily health metrics from the main dashboard:
- **Mood tracker**: Rate your emotional state (1–5 scale with emoji options)
- **Pain tracker**: Log pain location, severity (1–10), and type (cramps, headache, breast pain, incision pain)
- **Bleeding/discharge tracker**: For monitoring lochia progress postpartum
- **Sleep log**: Track hours and quality of sleep
- **Feed tracker**: Record breastfeeding sessions (time, duration, side)

### Symptom Checker
Use the Symptom Checker to understand potential causes of symptoms. The AI assistant will:
1. Ask about your symptoms in detail
2. Suggest whether the issue is mild, moderate, or needs emergency attention
3. Recommend appropriate actions (home care, doctor visit, emergency)

**Important**: The Symptom Checker is for guidance only. Always confirm with your doctor.

### Medication Reminders
Set up personalised reminder schedules for:
- Iron supplements
- Calcium supplements
- Folic acid / prenatal vitamins
- Doctor-prescribed medications
- Postpartum contraceptives

To add a medication:
1. Go to **Medications** in the bottom navigation
2. Tap **+ Add Medication**
3. Select the medication name, dosage, and frequency
4. Enable push notifications for reminders

---

## 3. AI Chat Assistant

The Afterma AI assistant (Afterma Sahayika) can answer:
- General postpartum recovery questions
- Breastfeeding guidance
- Nutrition advice for Indian mothers
- Information about government schemes
- Insights from Afterma research papers
- Platform usage questions

**To start a chat**: Tap the chat bubble icon on the home screen.

**Starting a new conversation**: Tap the trash icon to clear your session history.

The AI remembers context within your current session but **does not retain medical data** between sessions.

---

## 4. Doctor Consultation

### Booking an Appointment
1. Go to **Consult** tab in the navigation
2. Browse available doctors by specialty (gynecologist, lactation consultant, psychologist)
3. Select a time slot
4. Choose the consultation type: **Video call**, **Phone call**, or **In-clinic**
5. Pay securely via UPI, card, or net banking
6. Receive a confirmation with the meeting link or clinic address

### During the Consultation
- Your health logs are shared with the doctor (with your permission)
- You can share specific symptom entries directly from the Health Log
- After the consultation, the doctor may add prescription notes to your profile

### Post-Consultation
- Download your consultation summary from **History**
- Set new medication reminders based on doctor's advice
- Book a follow-up appointment directly

## 5. Community Support

### Afterma Community Forum
Connect with other Indian mothers in the community:
- **Trimester groups**: Connect with others at the same stage
- **C-section support**: Dedicated forums for C-section mothers
- **Breastfeeding help**: Share and receive breastfeeding support
- **PPD Support circle**: A safe, moderated space for mental health discussions

**Moderation**: All content is moderated by Afterma health educators. No medical advice is given in the community — all clinical questions are redirected to the AI assistant or doctor consultation.

---

## 6. Government Scheme Finder

Access the **Schemes** section to:
- Browse active central and state government maternal welfare schemes
- Check your eligibility with a guided questionnaire
- Get step-by-step application instructions
- Find nearest Anganwadi Centre or health facility

---

## 7. Reports and Analytics

The **Reports** section provides:
- **Mood trends**: Chart your emotional health over weeks/months
- **Pain patterns**: Identify recurring pain and share reports with your doctor
- **Recovery milestones**: Track progress against normal recovery timelines
- **Feed summary**: Total breastfeeding time, sessions per day
- **Exportable PDF reports**: Share with your doctor or for insurance records

---

## 8. Notifications and Privacy

### Notification Settings
Go to **Settings → Notifications** to configure:
- Medication reminders
- Appointment reminders
- Daily health log prompts
- Community replies

### Privacy
- You control what health data is shared with doctors
- Community posts are anonymous by default
- Data is encrypted and never sold
- Request data deletion anytime from **Settings → Account → Delete Data**

---

## 9. Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| AI not responding | Check internet connection; retry after a few seconds |
| Medication reminder not firing | Check notification permissions in phone Settings → Apps → Afterma |
| Can't book a consultation | Ensure your profile (name + phone) is complete |
| Chat history lost | Sessions reset on logout for privacy; use the same account login |
| App crashing | Update to the latest version from Play Store / App Store |
